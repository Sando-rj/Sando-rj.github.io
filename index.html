Currently looking for a Postdoctoral position, I am a research engineer at team LOKI, Inria Lille. I completed my Ph.D. Human-Computer Interaction at LISN (Université Paris-Saclay, Inria, CNRS), in February 2023. This thesis was done in the ILDA research team, under the supervision of Pr. Anastasia Bezerianos and Dr. Olivier Chapuis

My Ph.D. research explored the possibility for combination of AR and physical displays, in the context of collaboration and information visualization. It focused on using AR as a support for physical displays, to increase the possibilities for interaction and visualization. My research interests are Augmented/Virtual/Mixed Reality, and Human-Computer Interaction.
