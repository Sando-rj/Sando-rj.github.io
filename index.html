I am a PhD student in computer science at LRI, Université Paris-Saclay, part of the ILDA research teams. I work with Anastasia Bezerianos and Olivier Chapuis
My research interests are Augmented Reality, and Human-Computer Interaction. My current research explores the possibility for combination of AR and physical displays, in the context of collaboration and information visualization. I focus on using AR as a support for physical displays, to increase the possibilities for interaction and visualization.
